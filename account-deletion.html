Msari Member – Account Deletion Request

If you want to delete your Msari Member account and associated personal data, please request deletion through our support/staff team.

How to request deletion

  • Send an email to: henrik.palsson@previsions.com
  • Include your Member Number ID and registered mobile number/email for verification

What will be deleted

  • Account/profile data
  • Registration documents (photo ID/selfie), if provided

What may be retained

Transaction/audit records may be retained for compliance, fraud prevention, and accounting for up to 90 days period.

Processing time

We typically complete requests within 30 business days after verification.
