Msari Member – Privacy Policy
Effective date: 2026-05-28

This Privacy Policy explains how the Msari Member mobile application (“App”) collects, uses, shares, and protects information when you use the App.

1) Who we are
App name: Msari Member
Package name: com.msari.memberapp
Contact: henrik.palsson@previsions.com

2) Information we collect
We collect the following information to provide member account access, rewards, vouchers, and transfers:
Account & profile information
Member identifier (e.g., Member Number ID), name, and contact details you provide (such as mobile number or email).
Identity / registration documents
Photos you upload for registration such as photo ID and selfie.
Rewards and voucher activity
Reward points balance, voucher redemption history, reward transfers (sender/receiver IDs), and related transaction timestamps.
Device / app information
App version and basic device/network information required for reliability and troubleshooting.
Camera access
Used to scan QR codes (for reward transfer / recipient scanning). We do not collect photos from your camera unless you explicitly capture and submit them (e.g., registration documents).
Local app storage
The App stores some data on your device (for example, cached vouchers and sync data) to support offline use.

3) How we use your information
We use your information to:
Create and manage your member account and authenticate you
Provide rewards features (balance, recent activity)
Process voucher redemption and reward transfers
Sync your data between your device and our backend services
Prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
Maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the App

4) How we share your information
We may share information only as needed to operate the App:
With our backend service providers / infrastructure used to host and run the Msari Member services (e.g., databases, servers).
With authorized merchants/staff systems only to support reward/voucher operations tied to your membership.
For legal reasons if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.
We do not sell your personal information.

5) Data retention
We keep information only as long as necessary to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Some transactional records (such as voucher/reward history) may be retained for audit and accounting purposes.

6) Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including access controls and secure transmission. However, no method of storage or transmission is 100% secure.

7) Your choices and rights
Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to:
Access or correct your account information
Request deletion of your account and related personal data (subject to legal/operational retention needs)
Withdraw consent where applicable
To make a request, contact us at: henrik.palsson@previsions.com.

8) Children’s privacy
The App is intended for general members and is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

9) Third-party services
The App may use third-party libraries and services for networking, security, and app functionality. These providers may process limited data as required to provide their services.

10) Changes to this policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Effective date” and, if changes are significant, provide additional notice as required.
